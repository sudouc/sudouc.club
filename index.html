Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for?
Shoot an email to sudoexecutive@uni.canberra.edu.au and we'll get back to you.

I'm new to clubs. How can I join Sudo?
It’s easier than you think! Click here to join Sudo. Membership fee only cost $5 per year.
When are events run?
Sudo runs events every week. Check the event schedule to see what's coming up next
How will events be run?
We will be watching the pandemic closely, following all government and university guidelines. Any in-person events require you to register your attendance and follow social distancing requirements. In some cases we may be required to limit attendance at our events. In this case members get priority on a first-come-first-served basis
I'm studying remotely. Can I still join in?
Of course! Most our events will have a way to join remotely so all can take part
Why should I join Sudo?
Here's our top four reasons why you should join Sudo:
  • • Get to indulge in your passions and build your skills.
  • • Pursue your kickstarting ideas with resources available to you.
  • • Meet new people with similar interests to you and expand your network;
  • • and of course, to have fun!
How can I get more information about getting involved at Sudo?
It's easy! Attend our Annual General Meeting (AGM). Stay tuned for our next one. 👋
